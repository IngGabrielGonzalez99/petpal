Who is PetPal for Animales?

PetPal for Animals is a unique animal facility, nestled on 6.5 acres in the mountains of Western Maryland, near the vacation destinations of Deep Creek Lake and WISP Resort. We are a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to saving the lives of companion animals through rescue and adoption. Our operating model includes a full-service veterinarian practice, a pet hotel, and pet-grooming services, all within the center.

Did you know?

Petpal for Animals has rescued over 14,000 animals since our beginning in 2005
